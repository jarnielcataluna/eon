You've come up with so many ways to use EON for fun, for work, and everything in between!
We're taking a step back so you can keep doing just that.

Why
Co-Create?

Remember that orange card? The one with the eye? That’s us! Unionbank launched EON as an internet-based savings account for people who found requirements on opening account troublesome. Side-by-side with the leaps and bounds in the digital world, EON's users found a lot of other ways to use their accounts--may it be getting paid with Paypal, using debit, paying bills and so much more.

By taking cues from you, the new EON is here to provide the unbelievable experience you've been asking for. Grab your starter kit from the nearest convenient store, and have control over your account and enjoy an intuitive digital experience with the new EON app. EON is going social for customer service; reach us through Facebook or Twitter.

And this is just the beginning! We are all ears. Give us your ideas. EON will continue to evolve with each shout-out. All you need to do is ask, and together, we will make it possible.

In a very real way, you created the EON of today.

Your interactions with the online world comprise something we call "The Digital Me". All of our efforts go into helping The Digital Me live to the fullest.

EON is all about making life a breeze for that part of you.

“The more we give up to the users, the richer their experience becomes.”

Paolo Baltao

SVP Digital Transformation
UnionBank
