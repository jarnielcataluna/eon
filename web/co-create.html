Co-Create

Look back when the internet boomed, heralding the start of the new millennium. Before surfing online became a regular part of our daily lives, it was a cool trend back then — a craze! People started craving to buy stuff online, and EON made it so easy. Since then, the digital world has made leaps and bounds, with EON users discovering a bunch of ways to use their accounts.

They've opened new ways to earn and spend by linking their EON accounts to PayPal. Still others have gone cashless by using EON to pay at stores.

“The more we stepped back and gave up EON to the community, the more EON grew and moved forward”

Paolo Baltao
