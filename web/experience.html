Adding Funds to
Your EON Account is Just as Easy

Transfer Funds from PayPal
to Your EON Account

Moving money from your PayPal account to EON is a breeze! Just select UnionBank of the Philippines as the bank and enter its bank code for an easy transfer, which costs a minimal fee of PhP50. And when you're moving at least PhP7,000, withdrawing from PayPal is free!

Cash in at Convenience Stores

You don't need to take a trip to the bank to add funds to your EON account. Head to a nearby convenience store and you're as good as done, like buying a snack. There's a minimal fee of PhP10 for every PhP500 you cash in this way—still cheaper and no hassle than making a trip to the bank!

Security

  • Log in via Selfie or Touch ID

    Take a selfie to log in. Or you can use TouchID. Either way, forgetting your password will never be a problem!
  • Lock Your Account

    There's really no such thing as "too secure". As an extra measure, you can lock your account with the EON app or the EON website.
  • Change Your PIN

    You can also use the app to change your PIN. Looking for an ATM just to do that is such a waste of time!

Convenience

  • Free Mobile Data Access

    No WiFi? No problem! You can access the EON app using mobile data on any network for free as long as you're not on roaming!
  • Account Upgrade

    You can upgrade your EON account for a quick level-up the moment the need arises.
  • Card Replacement

    Losing your card really shouldn't be so troublesome. If you need another EON card, just buy a new one at a convenience store and link it to your account with the app. Because nobody has time for a long replacement process.

Services

  • Move money

  • Pay bills
