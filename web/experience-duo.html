Every Feature has Your Security in Mind

A worry-free online credit account that gives you full control of your purchases? You got it. With EON Duo, you're better protected from anyone who would misuse your money.

    • Lock your account anytime

      You can lock or unlock your account with the EON app or through the EON website. There's no need to head to a bank—you don't even need to call! Your security simply won't wait.
    • Dynamic CVV for security

      Let go of your fear of online credit card fraud. EON Duo lets you generate a new CVV with each online transaction to make sure that nobody charges your account apart from you.
    • Choose trusted merchants

      You can switch this feature on and off anytime with the app. It lets you screen the web merchants you'll allow transactions to. If you don't find your online store of choice in our list, simply nominate it!
